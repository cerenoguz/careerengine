01 / PRODUCT FLOW

A daily decision system, not another job board.

CareerEngine re-evaluates jobs observed during each scan rather than permanently hiding them after one appearance. It surfaces the strongest current matches while showing which postings are newly discovered.

01

Collect

Retrieves public postings from Greenhouse, Ashby, Lever, and Workable sources.

02

Evaluate

Removes clear authorization and experience mismatches, then identifies early-career signals.

03

Rank

Combines explainable technical-fit signals with bounded Sentence-BERT profile alignment.

04

Deliver

Emails the top 25 roles and includes the remaining ranked matches in an attachment.

04 / ENGINEERING DECISIONS

Hybrid ranking without an opaque black box.

Deterministic logic handles clear restrictions such as citizenship, clearance, and required experience. Semantic similarity contributes a limited, inspectable signal rather than making the final decision alone.

Explainable filtering

Evidence-based work-authorization and seniority checks keep hard constraints explicit.

Profile-aware ranking

Sentence-BERT measures semantic alignment with a technical profile while rule-based scoring preserves control.

Fresh daily ranking

Current jobs are re-ranked every scan; first-observed dates distinguish new postings from existing matches.

Operational visibility

Source-health reporting makes disabled, failed, and successfully collected sources visible in every report.
