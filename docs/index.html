WHY I BUILT THIS

Job searching was messy. I wanted to make it structured.

As a new Computer Science and Mathematics graduate, I was repeatedly searching the same company career sites, opening roles that looked promising, and trying to decide which ones were actually realistic for my background.

CareerEngine is my answer to that problem: scan broadly, filter carefully, rank transparently, and keep the final decision human.

“

The goal is not to replace judgment. It is to spend less time sorting noise and more time applying to opportunities worth pursuing.

02

HOW IT WORKS

A daily decision system, not another job board.

01

Collect

Retrieves public postings from Greenhouse, Ashby, Lever, and Workable career sources.

02

Filter

Removes clear work-authorization, location, and experience-level mismatches.

03

Rank

Combines technical fit, early-career signals, and Sentence-BERT profile similarity.

04

Deliver

Emails the top 25 current matches and keeps the remaining ranked roles available to review.

03

ENGINEERING DECISIONS

AI-assisted, but not an opaque black box.

Explainable constraints

Clear restrictions such as citizenship, clearance, and required experience are handled deterministically.

Profile-aware ranking

Sentence-BERT contributes a bounded semantic-fit signal rather than silently controlling the final rank.

Fresh daily context

Roles are re-ranked based on what is currently available, with first-observed dates showing what is new.

Operational visibility

Source-health reporting makes successful, failed, disabled, and unavailable sources visible every run.
